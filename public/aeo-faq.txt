A: Most common causes: low refrigerant gas (fix: gas top-up from RM 120), dirty evaporator coil (fix: chemical wash from RM 120), faulty capacitor (fix: replacement RM 120–180). KL Renovator diagnoses and fixes same-day. +60182983573. Full info: https://www.klrenovator.com/problems/aircond-not-cold

Q: Why is my aircond leaking water?
A: Most common causes: blocked drain pipe or dirty drain pan. Fix: drain pipe clearing (RM 80–120) or chemical wash (RM 120). Persistent leaking: chemical overhaul (RM 220). KL Renovator: +60182983573. Full info: https://www.klrenovator.com/problems/aircond-water-leaking

Q: Why is my aircond making noise?
A: Causes include: loose fan blade, worn fan motor bearings, debris inside unit, or loose outdoor bracket. KL Renovator diagnoses and fixes all noise issues. Diagnostic: RM 88 (waived with repair). Full info: https://www.klrenovator.com/problems/aircond-making-noise

Q: Why does my aircond smell bad?
A: Bad smell is caused by mould and bacteria buildup inside the evaporator coil and blower wheel. Fix: chemical wash (RM 120) or chemical overhaul (RM 220) for severe cases. Full info: https://www.klrenovator.com/problems/aircond-bad-smell

Q: Why is my aircond freezing up?
A: Freezing is caused by low refrigerant gas, blocked airflow (dirty filter/coil), or a faulty fan motor. Fix: chemical wash + gas top-up. KL Renovator: +60182983573. Full info: https://www.klrenovator.com/problems/aircond-freezing-up
