Q: What is included in a chemical overhaul?
A: KL Renovator's chemical overhaul includes: complete dismantle of the indoor unit from the wall, full chemical bath of the coil, blower wheel, drain pan and casing, drain channel clearing, unit reassembly, reinstallation, pressure test, cooling verification, and 1-month workmanship warranty. Takes 2–3 hours per unit.

Q: When should I get a chemical overhaul instead of a chemical wash?
A: Choose a chemical overhaul if: (1) water is still leaking after a chemical wash, (2) ice is forming on the coil or pipe, (3) the unit smells bad again within 2 months of a wash, (4) the unit has never been dismantled in 3+ years, (5) cooling is still weak after both gas top-up and chemical wash.
